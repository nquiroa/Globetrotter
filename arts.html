Top attractions in Providence

The Rhode Island School of Design Museum of Art

Welcome to the Rhode Island School of Design's Museum of Art, where creativity and innovation converge to inspire and captivate visitors from around the world. Nestled in the heart of Providence, Rhode Island, this renowned museum stands as a testament to the power of artistic expression and the boundless possibilities of human imagination. With a rich and diverse collection spanning centuries and continents, the RISD Museum offers a journey through history, culture, and creativity, inviting visitors to explore masterpieces of painting, sculpture, design, and more. From ancient artifacts to contemporary works, each piece tells a story, sparking curiosity and fostering a deeper understanding of the world around us.

PVDFest

Welcome to Providence's PVDFest, an electrifying celebration of art, culture, and community that transforms the city into a vibrant tapestry of creativity. Each year, PVDFest brings together artists, performers, and spectators from near and far to revel in a weekend filled with music, dance, food, and visual arts. From colorful parades to mesmerizing street performances, PVDFest showcases the diverse talents and cultural heritage that define Providence.

The Ghost Tours of Providence

Explore the eerie charm of Providence with us on the longest-standing ghost tour in the city! Join us for a uniquely haunting experience as we delve into the historical and paranormal mysteries of the College Hill neighborhood on Providence's East Side. Our tour is the culmination of seventeen years of meticulous historical research and spine-tingling encounters with the paranormal. Welcome to Providence Ghost Tour!
